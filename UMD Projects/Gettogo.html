GET TO GO

2024   |   UI/UX Design   |   Transportation Booking App

Overview

Get To Go is a smart travel and transportation mobile application designed to simplify daily commuting through a rechargeable travel card system and seamless digital ticket management. The platform allows users to recharge cards, manage travel expenses, track journeys, and access transportation services through a unified and user-friendly mobile experience.

This project was developed over four months as part of my UI/UX learning journey, involving extensive research, user analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and end-to-end mobile application design. The goal was to create a modern transit experience that reduces friction in public transportation while improving accessibility, convenience, and everyday usability for commuters.

My Role

I independently handled the complete UI/UX design process — from initial research and user journey mapping to wireframing, visual design, and interactive mobile app flows. The project focused on solving real-world commuting challenges through intuitive interactions, structured information architecture, and scalable design systems.

  • User research and transportation workflow analysis
  • Competitor study and feature planning
  • User persona and journey mapping
  • Information architecture and app flow creation
  • Wireframing and low-fidelity prototyping
  • High-fidelity mobile UI design
  • Travel card recharge and payment flow design
  • Dashboard, ticketing, and schedule experience design
  • Design system and visual consistency
  • User-centered interaction and usability planning
