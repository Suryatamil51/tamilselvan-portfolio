BEAUTYCARE WEBSITE

2025   |   UI/UX Design   |   Landing Page / Website

Overview

Beautycare is a modern skincare and wellness e-commerce landing page designed to create a clean, calming, and premium shopping experience. The project focuses on presenting beauty and self-care products through visually engaging layouts, intuitive navigation, and conversion-focused user flows.

The goal was to build a brand-driven interface that balances aesthetics with usability — helping users easily explore collections, discover products, and build trust through a seamless digital experience.

My Role

I independently handled the complete UI/UX design process — from visual direction and layout planning to high-fidelity interface design and responsive user experience structuring. The focus was on creating a minimal, elegant, and user-friendly e-commerce experience aligned with modern beauty and wellness brands.

  • User flow and landing page structure
  • Information architecture and content hierarchy
  • High-fidelity UI design
  • E-commerce experience design
  • Visual styling and brand consistency
  • Responsive web interface design
