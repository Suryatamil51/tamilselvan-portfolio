ORGANIC DELIGHT

2024   |   UI/UX Design   |   Organic Food Mobile App

Overview

Organic Delight is a mobile-first organic shopping application designed to simplify the discovery and purchase of healthy, sustainable products. The platform focuses on delivering a clean and intuitive user experience through seamless product browsing, personalized recommendations, and streamlined shopping flows.

This project involved extensive user research, competitor analysis, usability planning, and end-to-end mobile app design to create a trustworthy and user-friendly digital shopping experience. The goal was to balance modern e-commerce functionality with a minimal and wellness-focused interface that encourages effortless product exploration and purchasing.

My Role

I independently handled the complete UI/UX design process — from research and wireframing to high-fidelity mobile interface design and user flow creation. The focus was on designing a scalable and intuitive shopping experience that prioritizes usability, accessibility, and visual clarity.

  • User research and competitor analysis
  • User persona and journey mapping
  • Information architecture and app flow planning
  • Wireframing and low-fidelity prototyping
  • High-fidelity mobile UI design
  • E-commerce experience and checkout flow design
  • Design system and visual consistency
  • User-centered interaction and usability planning
