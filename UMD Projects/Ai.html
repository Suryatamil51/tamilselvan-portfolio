SUNLIGHT AI AUTOMATION

2025   |   UI/UX Design   |   AI Automation / Landing Page

Overview

The Sunlight AI landing page was redesigned to create a modern and engaging digital experience for showcasing AI-powered process discovery and RPA automation solutions. The interface focuses on presenting complex automation workflows in a clear, visually structured, and user-friendly manner.

The goal of this project was to improve content accessibility, simplify product communication, and create a professional web experience that effectively highlights automation features, integrations, and business value for enterprise users.

My Role

I worked on the complete UI/UX design process for the landing page — focusing on layout structuring, visual hierarchy, user flow planning, and high-fidelity interface design. The objective was to design a clean, scalable, and conversion-focused experience aligned with modern SaaS and AI product standards.

  • Landing page UI/UX design
  • Information architecture and content hierarchy
  • Wireframing and layout structuring
  • High-fidelity web interface design
  • Visual consistency and responsive experience planning
  • User-focused SaaS product presentation design
