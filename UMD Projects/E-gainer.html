E-GAINER LEARNING PLATFORM

2024   |   UI/UX Design   |   Educational Platform / Landing Page

Overview

E-Gainer is an online learning platform designed to help students and professionals gain industry-focused skills through accessible and career-oriented education. The platform focuses on creating a structured and engaging learning experience by showcasing courses, certifications, live sessions, and job-focused programs within a modern digital interface.

The goal of this project was to design a user-friendly educational platform that simplifies course discovery, improves content accessibility, and builds trust through clear information hierarchy and intuitive user journeys.

My Role

I worked on the complete UI/UX design process for the platform — focusing on layout planning, user flow structuring, visual hierarchy, and high-fidelity interface design. The objective was to create an engaging and scalable learning experience that balances usability, accessibility, and modern educational design standards.

  • Learning platform UI/UX design
  • Information architecture and course flow planning
  • High-fidelity web interface design
  • Educational dashboard and content structuring
  • Visual consistency and responsive experience design
  • User-centered learning experience planning
