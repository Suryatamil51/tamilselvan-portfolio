SAVING STAR

2024   |   UI/UX Design   |   Finance Management Mobile App

Overview

Saving Star is a personal savings and financial management mobile application designed to help users build better saving habits through goal-based planning and intuitive money tracking. The platform enables users to manage savings goals, monitor financial progress, and organize expenses through a simple and user-friendly mobile experience.

This project focused on creating a clean and approachable fintech experience that simplifies personal finance management for everyday users. The goal was to design an interface that balances usability, trust, and clarity while encouraging consistent financial engagement through structured user flows and accessible interactions.

My Role

I independently handled the complete UI/UX design process — from research and user flow planning to wireframing and high-fidelity mobile interface design. The focus was on building a modern fintech experience that feels simple, organized, and visually engaging for users managing their financial goals.

  • User research and competitor analysis
  • User persona and journey mapping
  • Financial goal and savings flow planning
  • Wireframing and low-fidelity prototyping
  • High-fidelity mobile UI design
  • Dashboard and transaction experience design
  • Design system and visual consistency
  • User-centered interaction and usability planning
